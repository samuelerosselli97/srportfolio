Da Amelia Restaurant - 4K WiFi Surveillance

Project Overview

Deployed a state-of-the-art 4K WiFi surveillance system at Ristorante Da Amelia featuring 15 wireless cameras with professional cable management. The system provides ultra-high-definition monitoring with the flexibility of wireless connectivity.

System Specifications

Cameras: 15x 4K UHD WiFi IP Cameras
Resolution: 4K Ultra HD (3840 x 2160)
Connectivity: Full WiFi 5GHz Network
NVR: 16-Channel 4K Network Video Recorder
Storage: 60-Day 4K Recording Archive
Night Vision: Advanced IR (30m range)
Remote Access: Mobile & Desktop Apps
AI Features: Motion Detection & Alerts

Coverage Areas

  • Main dining room and customer seating
  • Kitchen and food preparation areas
  • Bar and cashier stations
  • Entrance, exit, and emergency routes
  • Storage rooms and back areas
  • Outdoor patio and parking area

Installation Process

  • Site Assessment: Comprehensive survey of restaurant layout and WiFi signal strength analysis
  • Network Optimization: Deployed dedicated 5GHz WiFi network for camera traffic
  • Camera Installation: Mounted 15 4K WiFi cameras at strategic locations
  • Cable Management: Professional cable channels for clean, organized power routing
  • System Configuration: Paired all cameras to NVR with optimized 4K recording settings
  • Fine-Tuning: Adjusted angles, exposure, and motion zones for each camera
  • Remote Setup: Configured mobile app access for manager oversight
  • Staff Training: Comprehensive training on playback, export, and system management

Key Advantages

  • 4K Ultra HD crystal-clear footage
  • Wireless installation - minimal drilling
  • Easy camera relocation if needed
  • Professional cable channel management
  • 7-day rolling video archive (expandable if necessary)
