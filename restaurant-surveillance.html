Strandbad Restaurant Surveillance

10-Camera HD Security System · NVR Configuration

Project Overview

Designed and installed a comprehensive security surveillance system with 10 high-resolution cameras and advanced NVR configuration for the Strandbad Restaurant. The system ensures 24/7 monitoring of all critical areas to protect staff and guests alike.

System Specifications

Cameras: 10x High-Resolution IP Cameras
Resolution: Full HD / 4K
NVR: Advanced Network Video Recorder
Storage: 7+-Day Rolling Archive with expansion possibility
Night Vision: IR Illumination
Detection: Motion Alerts & AI Zones
Remote Access: Mobile & Desktop Apps

Coverage Areas

  • Main dining room and customer seating
  • Kitchen and food preparation areas
  • Bar and cashier stations
  • Main entrance and exit points
  • Outdoor terrace and beach access
  • Storage and back-of-house areas

Installation Process

  • Site Survey: Comprehensive assessment of premises and optimal camera placement planning
  • Network Setup: Configured dedicated security VLAN for isolated camera traffic
  • Hardware Installation: Mounted 10 cameras with weatherproof housing and clean cable management
  • Camera Configuration: Optimized angles, exposure, and motion detection zones for each unit
  • NVR Setup: Configured recording schedules, storage allocation, and retention policies
  • Remote Access: Enabled secure mobile and desktop monitoring with manager credentials
  • Staff Training: Comprehensive training on playback, export, and system management
  • Testing & Validation: Verified full coverage, recording quality, and system reliability

Key Features

  • Weatherproof outdoor cameras
  • Wide Dynamic Range (WDR) for varying light conditions
  • Motion-triggered alerts and notifications
  • Continuous 24/7 recording
  • Encrypted data transmission
